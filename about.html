Why Accessibility?

As I grew up, technology has increasingly become more crucial to my daily life. With this, I have seen my step father's relationship with technology adapt and change as well. My step dad has hearing loss and requires hearing aids. For a long time, he only used Android phones and technology, but has since switched to iPhone. After switching, he has been able to incorporate his hearing aids into his technology and use it for assistive listening. Witnessing my step father's journey with technology and how his accessibility needs have impacted that experience, accessibility has become a major foucs for myself as I venture through technology and learning.
